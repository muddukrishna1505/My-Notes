DNS setup:
=========
1.Purchase a domain from Godaddy/Hostineger(web hosting providers)
2.Go to aws then search for Route53(DNS service) then select Hosted Zones click on Create Hosted Zone
give domain name(it should be same as your purchased domain) ex:bmkrishtech.xyz click on create.
Then you will see record name bmkrishtech.xyz and record type A some nameservers
3.Open your domain ex: bmkrishtech.xyz thne click on internet icon -> nameservers then click nameservers becauser we are adding nameservers from aws(our infra will be in aws so)
